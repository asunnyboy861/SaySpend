Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log an expense?

Tap the microphone button on the home screen and simply say what you spent. For example, "I spent $5.50 on coffee at Starbucks." SaySpend will automatically detect the amount, category, and merchant.

Can I add expenses manually?

Yes! Tap the + button in the top right corner to manually enter an expense with amount, category, merchant, and notes.

How do I scan a receipt?

Tap the + button, then select "Scan Receipt." You can take a photo or choose one from your photo library. SaySpend uses OCR to extract the expense details automatically.

What categories are available?

SaySpend includes 10 built-in categories: Coffee, Food, Transport, Shopping, Entertainment, Health, Bills, Education, Travel, and Other.

Subscription FAQ

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find SaySpend and tap it
  5. Choose "Cancel Subscription" or change your plan

Or visit: https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions

How do I restore my purchases?

Open SaySpend, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases".

What's the difference between Monthly and Yearly?

Both plans offer the same premium features including budget management, iCloud sync, and full statistics. The Yearly plan offers a 44% discount compared to paying monthly.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You can continue using premium features until the end of your current billing period. After that, you'll revert to the free version with basic expense tracking.

I purchased Lifetime but it's not showing

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore your Lifetime access.

How do I set a budget?

Go to Settings, then tap "Budgets." You can set monthly spending limits for each category. Premium users get budget alerts when approaching their limits.

Does SaySpend sync across devices?

Yes! Premium users can enable iCloud Sync in Settings to keep expenses updated across all Apple devices signed into the same Apple ID.
