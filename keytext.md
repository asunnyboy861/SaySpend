# App Store Metadata

## Promotional Text
Track expenses by voice, scan receipts, and manage budgets effortlessly. Say it, snap it, done.

## Description
SaySpend is the simplest way to track your daily expenses. Just speak naturally and your spending is logged instantly. No more tedious manual entry.

VOICE-POWERED TRACKING
Say things like "I spent 5 dollars on coffee" or "Lunch was 12 bucks at the deli" and SaySpend captures the amount, category, and merchant automatically. Hands-free expense logging has never been easier.

RECEIPT SCANNING
Snap a photo of any receipt and SaySpend uses OCR to extract the amount, merchant name, and date. No typing required. Just point, shoot, and save.

SMART CATEGORIES
Ten built-in categories including Coffee, Food, Transport, Shopping, Entertainment, Health, Bills, Education, Travel, and Other. SaySpend auto-detects the right category from your voice or receipt input.

BEAUTIFUL STATISTICS
See exactly where your money goes with clean charts and monthly breakdowns. Understand your spending patterns at a glance. Free users see a 7-day view. Premium unlocks full monthly and yearly trends.

BUDGET MANAGEMENT
Set monthly spending limits for each category and get alerts before you overspend. Stay on track with visual progress bars that show remaining budget at a glance.

iCLOUD SYNC
Enable iCloud Sync to keep your expenses updated across all your Apple devices. Your data stays in your personal iCloud account. We never see it.

DATA EXPORT
Export your expense data to CSV format for use in spreadsheets or tax preparation. Your data, your way.

SIMPLE AND PRIVATE
No bank login required. No account creation needed. All data stays on your device or in your personal iCloud. We do not collect or store any personal information.

WHY SAYSPEND?
Unlike complex finance apps with overwhelming features, SaySpend focuses on speed and simplicity. Log an expense in under 3 seconds with your voice. That is it. No clutter, no confusion.

SaySpend is perfect for anyone who wants to track spending without the hassle of traditional budgeting apps. Whether you are a student managing a tight budget, a freelancer tracking business expenses, or simply someone who wants to know where your money goes, SaySpend makes it effortless.
